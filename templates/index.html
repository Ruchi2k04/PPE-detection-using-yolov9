Abstract

Safety Guards Detection is a comprehensive industrial safety and surveillance system designed to enhance workplace safety and security. Leveraging advanced computer vision and deep learning, it ensures worker safety by detecting and promoting the correct usage of safety guards (helmet, safety vests, boots). The purpose of Safety Guard Detection is to enhance safety and security in industrial environments through advanced surveillance and monitoring. It relies on computer vision techniques, including object detection and recognition, to analyze input data from various sources. Deep learning models, such as YOLO (You Only Look Once), play a pivotal role in identifying workers' safety guard items. These models accurately determine if workers are wearing helmets, safety vests, and boots

Key Words: Computer vision, Object detection , Object recognition, Workers safety, Deep learning, Yolo v9, Safety Guards.
